DESIGNING WITH FRAMEWORKS

Learning to work with frameworks

After a few hours lecture where we saw examples, pros and cons of different frameworks - our task for this assignment was to style a landingpage using only a framework. Throughout that day we had speed-courses in Bootstrap and Pico pirmarly, and therefor the natural choise was to use of them for this assignment. I chose to use Pico, because this was a framework I had never worked with previosly. Pico is a framework based on the semantics of the HTML, and therefor I hade to add a lot of spesific sematic tags, in addition to divs, to make the framework work as it should.
